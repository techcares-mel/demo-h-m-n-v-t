Về Chúng Tôi · Our Story

Hẻm Nhỏ,
Hương Vị Lớn

HẺM ĂN VẶT — meaning Snack Alley — brings the vibrant flavours of Vietnamese street food culture to the heart of Sunshine North.

HẺM ĂN VẶT is a Vietnamese street food and snack restaurant located in Sunshine North, Melbourne. We bring the vibrant flavours of Vietnamese street food culture to the local community, serving a range of traditional Vietnamese snacks, casual dishes, and authentic street food favourites inspired by the laneways of Vietnam. From the crunch of freshly baked bánh mì to the sweet warmth of chè on a cool Melbourne evening — every dish is made with care, using time-honoured recipes passed down through generations.
