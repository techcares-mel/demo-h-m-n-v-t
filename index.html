Our Story

A Little Alley,
A World of Flavour

We bring the flavours of Vietnam's beloved street alleys to Sunshine North. From crispy bánh mì to refreshing chè, every dish is made with heart.

HẺM ĂN VẶT — meaning "Snack Alley" — is inspired by the vibrant laneways of Hanoi and Hồ Chí Minh City, where locals gather after dark over steaming bowls, grilled skewers, and sweet cold drinks. We've brought those same recipes, that same warmth, to the heart of Sunshine North's food community at Berkshire Road.
