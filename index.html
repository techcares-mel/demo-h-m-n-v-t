Who We Are

Snack Alley, Reimagined

HẺM ĂN VẶT — meaning "Snack Alley" — brings the vibrant flavours of Vietnamese street food culture to the heart of Sunshine North, Melbourne.

We serve a range of traditional Vietnamese snacks, casual dishes, and authentic street food favourites inspired by the laneways of Vietnam. From the crunch of a freshly baked bánh mì roll to the sweet chill of chè on a warm afternoon — every dish is made fresh, daily, with genuine care and time-honoured recipes.
